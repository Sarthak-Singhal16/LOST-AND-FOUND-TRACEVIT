Frequently Asked Questions

To report a lost item, log into your TRACEVIT account and click on "Report Lost Item" button on your dashboard. Fill in the required details about your item, including when and where you lost it, a description, and upload any photos if available.

The time to locate lost items varies based on several factors including the accuracy of information provided, location of loss, and whether someone has found and reported it. Some items may be found within hours, while others might take days or weeks. We continue searching until the item is found or for up to 90 days.

Basic services like reporting lost items and searching the database are free. Premium services, such as active search assistance and priority handling, come with nominal fees. Check our pricing page for detailed information about our service tiers and associated costs.

When your item is found, you'll receive a notification through your preferred contact method. To claim it, you'll need to verify ownership by providing additional details about the item that only the owner would know. Once verified, you can arrange for pickup or delivery of your item.
